About Us

We are the world's largest travel agency

With a combined experience of 16 years, Traveltavern offers the best available airfares, accommodation and migration consultancy rates.
Our clients enjoy the service and knowledge of a boutique business with the pricing benefits & security of a global organisation.

The award winning business is owned and operated by its management with experience in the travel industry respectively.
We have a team of experienced consultants, passionate about travel and committed to delivering outstanding service to their clients.
We are goal orientated and focused on achieving increased savings and efficiency for our business travelers.
Our global alliances and partners provide us with strong buying power and our innovative solutions.
Travel Tavern can become a valuable extension of your business.
